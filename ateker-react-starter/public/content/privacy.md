At Ateker, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency in how your personal information is handled. This Privacy Policy describes how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our website and related services.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### a. Information You Provide Voluntarily
- Name, email address, and organization when you sign up for updates or express interest in our products.
- Any messages or correspondence you send to us (e.g., via contact forms or email).

### b. Information Collected Automatically
- IP address, browser type, operating system
- Pages visited, time spent on pages, and referring URLs
- Device identifiers and usage data

### c. Cookies and Similar Technologies
We use cookies to improve your browsing experience, analyze traffic, and personalize content. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some features may not function properly.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use your data to:
- Provide, operate, and maintain our website and services
- Respond to your inquiries and communicate updates
- Analyze usage patterns and improve our platform
- Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security issues

We do **not** sell your data to third parties.

---

## 3. Legal Basis for Processing

We process your personal data on the following legal bases:
- Your consent
- Our legitimate interest in improving our services
- To comply with legal obligations

---

## 4. Data Sharing

We may share your data with:
- Service providers and partners who help us operate our platform (e.g., analytics tools)
- Authorities, if required by law or to protect legal rights

All third parties are contractually bound to protect your information.

---

## 5. Data Security

We implement technical and organizational measures to safeguard your data, including:
- Encryption during data transmission (HTTPS)
- Secure servers and limited access controls
- Regular audits and vulnerability testing

---

## 6. Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy, or as required by law.

---

## 7. Your Rights

You have the right to:
- Access the personal data we hold about you
- Request correction or deletion of your data
- Withdraw your consent at any time
- Lodge a complaint with a data protection authority

To exercise your rights, please contact us at [privacy@ateker.co](mailto:privacy@ateker.co).

## 8. International Data Transfers

If you are located outside the United States, your data may be transferred to and processed in the United States where our servers are located. We ensure such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws.

## 9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware of such data, we will delete it promptly.
